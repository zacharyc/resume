Goal:

I'm looking to find a way to volunteer some of my time to help a program or multiple programs develop. Through my 25 years of cheerleading experience, I've had some great coaches who have helped me develop and I'm looking to pay it forward.

My philosophy about cheerleading is based in the root of the word, leading fans in cheers. Progressions through motions, jumps, time and cheers are foremost. Learning safety progression of tumbling and stunting comes in second. I'm a strong proponent of safety first, and have been ACCAA certified for most of my cheerleading career (currently lapsed, but would consider taking it again).
